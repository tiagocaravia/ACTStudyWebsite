# QuizMe.ACT.org - Official ACT Practice Platform

## What is QuizMe?

**QuizMe** (https://quizme.act.org/) is an **official ACT platform** that provides:
- Real ACT practice questions
- Questions across all ACT subjects (English, Math, Reading, Science)
- Official ACT content and format

## Can You Use It for Your Website?

### ✅ **YES, but with proper authorization:**

1. **Contact ACT for Licensing**
   - Reach out to ACT's business development/licensing team
   - Discuss partnership opportunities
   - Inquire about:
     - API access to questions
     - Licensing agreements for educational use
     - Bulk question data access
     - Partnership programs

2. **Potential Benefits**
   - Access to official, high-quality ACT questions
   - Authentic question formats and difficulty levels
   - Regular updates with new questions
   - Official ACT endorsement (if partnership is established)

### ❌ **NO - Don't do this:**

- **DO NOT scrape** questions from QuizMe
- **DO NOT** manually copy questions
- **DO NOT** use questions without permission
- This violates:
  - ACT's Terms of Service
  - Copyright law
  - Could result in legal action

## How to Contact ACT

### For Licensing/Partnership Inquiries:

1. **ACT Business Development**
   - Website: https://www.act.org/
   - Look for "Partnerships" or "Business Development" sections
   - Contact form or email for licensing inquiries

2. **ACT Education Services**
   - They handle educational partnerships
   - May have programs for educational technology companies

3. **General Contact**
   - Phone: Check ACT.org for current contact information
   - Email: Look for business/partnership email addresses

## Alternative Approach

While waiting for ACT's response, you can:

1. **Use Official Free Practice Tests**
   - Download from ACT.org (free practice tests)
   - These are typically allowed for educational use
   - Verify terms of use for each test

2. **Create Your Own Questions**
   - Write ACT-style questions based on:
     - ACT content specifications
     - Official ACT question formats
     - ACT difficulty levels
   - This is 100% legal and safe

3. **Partner with Educational Publishers**
   - Some publishers have licensed ACT-style question banks
   - May offer better licensing terms

## Recommended Action Plan

1. **Immediate**: Start with official free practice tests from ACT.org
2. **Short-term**: Create your own ACT-style questions
3. **Long-term**: Contact ACT about QuizMe licensing/partnership
4. **Ongoing**: Build your question database while pursuing official partnerships
